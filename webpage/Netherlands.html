Trust in the Netherlands

Now we switch the focus from trust in Europe to trust in the Netherlands. On the left side the home burglary rates from 2012 to 2017 are shown. As you can see, they are decreasing. So maybe the 'touwtje in de deur' was not such a good idea? On the right side the barchart shows the trust percentages in 2012, as measured by the CBS. When you click on a certain bar in this chart, it will set the percentages of trust in that certain variable out againt the home burglary rate. Below this text, a linegaph is showing the different crimerates. You can adjust the graph to your own likings. Next to the crime rates a rapport from the CBS is shown, explaining (in Dutch) why the crimerites are decreasing, for those interested.
