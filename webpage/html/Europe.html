EU defined by Trust in Humanity

Trust in Europe

The European Union came forth from the European Coal and Steel Community. A community build after the second world war, to prevent a new war from happening - as for war you'll need coal and steel. The ECSC developped further into the contemporary EU. But with the Brexit, the unrest in Spain and Turkey - it seems that the European Deficit, the gap between the citizen and the leaders in Europe, is increasing. But is there really a lack of trust between them? Judge yourself: chose a year from which you like to see data, click on a country and see how the citizens rate their feeling of trust on a scale from 1 to 10.
